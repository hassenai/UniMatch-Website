About UniMatch

About UniMatch

At UniMatch, we specialize in providing tailored services to connect international students with some of the most prestigious universities in the UK.

Our mission is to simplify the process of studying abroad by offering comprehensive support from the very first step of the university application journey to settling into life in the UK.
We understand that the path to studying abroad can be complex, with various challenges along the way. That's why we offer expert guidance on every aspect of your educational journey. Our services include university selection, application assistance, visa support, and housing solutions. Whether you're seeking advice on choosing the right university based on your academic and personal goals or need help navigating the UK visa process, we are here to make your experience smooth and stress-free.

In addition to helping you get into your dream university, we provide ongoing support to ensure that you're fully prepared for life in the UK. This includes assistance with accommodation arrangements, finding part-time job opportunities, and offering advice on student life. Our commitment doesn't end once you’ve received your offer — we support you throughout your entire journey, from securing your place at university to your arrival in the UK and beyond.

FAQs

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services including free university admission assistance, guidance on selecting the best university, visa support, housing assistance, and pre-departure briefings to prepare you for life in the UK.

How can I apply for your services?

To get started, simply email us at support@unimatch.org or send us a message on WhatsApp. Our team will get in touch with you shortly to guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

Is your university admission service free?

Yes! Our university admission service is completely free of charge. We are dedicated to helping you secure your place at the right university.

How long does the admission process take?

The admission process can vary depending on the university and the program you are applying for. Typically, it takes a few weeks to a few months. We work closely with you to ensure everything is submitted on time and correctly.

Do you assist with housing arrangements?

Yes, we help students find safe and affordable accommodation in the UK. We guide you through the different housing options and help you secure a place that fits your needs and budget.

What are Pre-Departure Briefings?

Our Pre-Departure Briefings provide students with essential information on living in the UK, including cultural differences, practical tips, and how to navigate your new environment with ease. It's a great way to prepare for your journey ahead!
